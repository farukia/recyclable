Privacy Policy for The Recycle Disciple

Effective Date: 5/26/25

The Recycle Disciple is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and store information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Recycling Data: We collect and store the number of items you recycle and your recycling goals to help track your progress and encourage environmentally friendly habits.
  • Camera Access: The app uses your camera to scan plastic Resin Identification Codes (RICs). No images are stored or uploaded—they are used only temporarily to identify the code and are discarded immediately after processing.

2. What We Don’t Collect

  • We do not collect or store any personal information.
  • We do not store, upload, or share the images you take.
  • We do not track your location or usage beyond recycling goals and item counts.

3. Data Storage

All recycling goals and item counts are stored locally on your device. This information is never shared with third parties or uploaded to any server.

4. Permissions

To function, The Recycle Disciple requires access to your device's camera. No other permissions are needed.

5. Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the effective date above and notify users as appropriate.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@sciologycamp.org.
